Crock Pot Angel Chicken

Ingredients

4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts

1 packet Italian Dressing Mix 0.7 oz packet

2 tsp Minced Garlic

1 can Cream of Chicken Soup 10.5 oz can

¾ cup Chicken Broth

4 oz Cream Cheese room temperature

16 oz Angel Hair Pasta cooked

Instructions

1. Place the chicken in the bottom of a crock pot.

2. Next add the wet ingredients – mashed bananas, cashew butter, almond milk, pure maple syrup, and vanilla extract to a large mixing bowl. Combine.

3. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or on high for 3 hours until the chicken is cooked through.

4. Dice the cream cheese and stir it into the crock pot. Cover and cook on low for 30 minutes or on high for 15 minutes until the cheese is melted.

5. Stir thoroughly to combine the cream cheese into the sauce in the crock pot.

6. Serve the chicken and sauce over the cooked angel hair pasta. Enjoy!
