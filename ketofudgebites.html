Keto Fudge Bites

Ingredients

1 cup unsweetened almond butter

1/2 cup coconut oil, melted

1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

1/4 cup powdered erythritol or your preferred keto-friendly sweetener

1 tsp vanilla extract

Optional: chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or sea salt for toppings

Instructions

1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the almond butter and melted coconut oil. Microwave for about 30 seconds, or until the mixture is slightly softened and easier to stir.

2. Add the cocoa powder, powdered sweetener, and vanilla extract to the bowl. Stir well until all the ingredients are thoroughly combined and the mixture is smooth.

3. Line a small baking dish or container with parchment paper. Pour the fudge mixture into the lined dish and spread it evenly using a spatula.

4. Optional: Sprinkle your choice of chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or a pinch of sea salt on top of the fudge mixture for added flavor and texture.

5. Place the dish in the refrigerator and let it chill for at least 2 hours, or until the fudge is firm and set.

6. Once the fudge is firm, remove it from the refrigerator and cut it into bite-sized pieces.

7. Serve and enjoy! Store any leftover fudge bites in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.
